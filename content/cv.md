Principal Research Engineer at Cloudflare working on privacy-preserving authentication for the web.

## Experience

**Cloudflare** — London, then Remote · May 2019 – present\
*Principal Research Engineer (2025–) · Research Engineer (2020–2025) · Systems Engineer (2019–2020)*

- Lead research on Privacy Pass and bot authentication; upgraded Cloudflare's deployment to the IETF standard, the protocol behind Private Access Tokens. It serves billions of requests a day.
- Initiated Web Bot Auth, cryptographic verification of bot and agent traffic with HTTP message signatures. Major CDN, cloud, and AI providers now run independent implementations.
- Built anonymous-credential rate limiting for bots and agents without compromising user privacy.
- Shipped key transparency auditing for WhatsApp's end-to-end encrypted messaging: public dashboard, Real World Crypto talk, and follow-up standardisation work.
- Co-designed the Cryptographic Attestation of Personhood, a CAPTCHA alternative studied in a SOUPS 2022 paper.
- Designed and implemented lock-free probabilistic caching in Cloudflare's CDN.
- Mentor team members, and have written more than 15 posts for the [Cloudflare blog](https://blog.cloudflare.com/author/thibault/).

**LiquidChain GmbH** — Zug, Switzerland · Mar 2018 – Apr 2019\
*Research Engineer* — distributed-ledger research and prototyping.

**CERN** — Geneva, Switzerland · Jun – Sep 2017\
*Intern* — built a developer-tools web portal in React.

**Isode Ltd** — Hampton, UK · Jun – Sep 2016\
*Software Engineering Intern* — built a Unicode-compliant emoji keyboard and TCL-based testing for XMPP.
